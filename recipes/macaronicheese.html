Mac n' Cheese Recipe

Description:

Boiled pasta drizzled in authentic Italian cheese sauce,
served with a side of garlic bread to really compliment the dish and treat your tastebuds.

Ingredients

  • Fusilli pasta
  • Italian cheddar cheese
  • Garlic bread
  • Butter

Instructions

  1. Boil 200g of fusilli pasta for 13m
  2. Turn off heat and drain water
  3. Mix pasta with cheese sauce and 1tsp of butter
  4. Mix until it is a liquid then heat for a further 4m
  5. Cook garlic bread for 3m
  6. Dish up food.
  7. Enjoy your meal!
