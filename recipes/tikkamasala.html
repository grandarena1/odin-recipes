Tikka Masala Recipe

Description:

Tender chicken lathered in a rich and creamy tikka masala sauce,
served with a side of naan breads and poppadoms to overload your tastebuds.

Ingredients

  • Chicken (diced)
  • Tikka masala sauce
  • Naan bread (x2)
  • Poppadoms (x2)

Instructions

  1. Dice chicken and tenderise
  2. Mix tikka masala sauce with chicken in pan
  3. Cook chicken for 35m
  4. Dish up chicken
  5. Soak naan bread in water and microwave for 1m30s
  6. Dish up naan bread and poppadoms
  7. Enjoy your meal!
